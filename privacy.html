Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

At LunchGenie, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our feedback form, you voluntarily provide your email address and message content. This information is collected solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry and improving our services.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website address
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • IP address (anonymized where possible)

1.3 Camera Data (AI Pet Detector)

When you use the AI Pet Detector feature, your device's camera captures images for real-time classification. These images are processed entirely within your web browser using TensorFlow.js. No camera data, images, or video are transmitted to our servers or any third party. All AI processing occurs locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Feedback responses: To read and respond to messages submitted through our feedback form.
  • Service improvement: To understand how users interact with our features and identify areas for improvement.
  • Technical maintenance: To ensure our website functions properly across different browsers and devices.

3. Third-Party Services

LunchGenie uses the following third-party services:

3.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3.2 Formspree

Our feedback form is powered by Formspree, a third-party form processing service. When you submit feedback, your email address and message are transmitted to Formspree's servers for delivery to us. Please review Formspree's Privacy Policy for details on their data handling practices.

3.3 Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to serve the Poppins typeface. When you visit our website, your browser may connect to Google's servers to load font files. This connection may transmit your IP address to Google.

3.4 TensorFlow.js and Teachable Machine

Our AI Pet Detector uses TensorFlow.js and Google's Teachable Machine libraries loaded from CDN servers. The machine learning model runs entirely in your browser. No image data is sent to any external server during classification.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Advertising cookies: Set by Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements.
  • Analytics cookies: To help us understand website usage patterns.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on our website.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our website (such as Formspree for form processing).
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • With your explicit consent.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out: Opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ads Settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our Contact page.

8. Children's Privacy

LunchGenie is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us through our Contact page or use the feedback form on our homepage.
