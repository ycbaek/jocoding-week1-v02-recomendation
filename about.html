About LunchGenie

LunchGenie is a free, web-based tool designed to help you make better lunch decisions every day. Whether you're stuck in a rut eating the same thing or simply can't decide among dozens of options, we're here to make your midday meal a little more exciting.

Our Mission

We believe that lunch should be something to look forward to, not stress about. Our mission is to take the decision fatigue out of mealtime by providing smart, randomized recommendations across a wide range of cuisines. We want to help people discover new foods, make healthier choices, and enjoy the process of picking what to eat.

Why LunchGenie Exists

The idea for LunchGenie started from a simple observation: every day, millions of people spend an unnecessary amount of time trying to figure out what to eat for lunch. Decision fatigue is a real phenomenon - the more choices we face throughout the day, the harder each subsequent decision becomes.

LunchGenie addresses this by narrowing down your options and introducing an element of surprise. Instead of scrolling through endless restaurant menus or delivery apps, you can use our quick picker to get an instant suggestion. The cuisine filters let you stay in control while still leaving room for pleasant surprises.

Our Approach to Content

Every meal in our database is carefully curated with accurate descriptions, realistic calorie estimates, and approximate preparation times. We focus on popular, widely available dishes that you can find at most restaurants or easily prepare at home. Our goal is to provide genuinely useful information, not just random suggestions.

Technology

LunchGenie is built with modern web technologies for fast performance and broad compatibility:

  • Frontend: Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with Web Components for modular UI elements.
  • AI Features: Google's Teachable Machine and TensorFlow.js enable browser-based machine learning without server dependencies.
  • Design: Responsive, mobile-first design using CSS custom properties and modern layout techniques.
  • Privacy: No tracking scripts, no user accounts, and no data collection beyond voluntary feedback submissions.

Our Values
